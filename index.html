Dr. Sarita Khandelwal is a Reproductive Medicine specialist and Best Gynecologist in Bhopal trained in the best institute of India, Christian Medical College, Vellore. She has expertise in Reproductive medicine and minimally invasive reproductive surgery. Reproductive Medicine deals with hormonal disorders of males and females including subfertility and includes assisted reproductive technologies like IVF, ICSI, Egg donation and gamut of reproductive tract surgeries like laparoscopy and hysteroscopy. Gynecologist deals with the health of the female reproductive system. Dr. Sarita Khandelwal is the lady gynecologist in Bhopal, who has expertise in the gynecological treatment.

Infertility

Infertility is stressful condition. While it is not considered a disease, it causes lot of mental and psychological upheavals. It also leads to social isolation and withdrawal especially in traditional societies.

Laparoscopic Myomectomy

Fibroids or myomas are common benign tumors in women. Almost 20% of women with infertility have fibroids. If the fibroid is more than 4 cm or is inside the lining of uterus ( submucous) it can affect fertility and needs to be removed.
